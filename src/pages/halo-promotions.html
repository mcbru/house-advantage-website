HALo Promotions

Automate Loyalty Campaign Management

HALo Promotions enable local and corporate teams to create, execute and manage promotional calendars to maintain a steady flow of fun and engaging campaigns and events for loyalty members. It offers a large library of promotional engines that can be tailored to seasonal offers, birthdays, anniversaries, sporting events or customized to a unique campaign. Trigger awards using a variety of “play X, get Y” scenarios and business rules to drive business and behavior.

HALo Promotions are flexible and easy-to-use, allowing non-technical users to manage a large library of promotions and awards. Promotions may be extended across one or more properties, and can include all customers, or a limited set of invited guests. Customers can participate in promotions through employee interaction, or directly through kiosks, mobile applications, or web browsers. Every touch point can make an impact with your customers and provide a reason for returning to your program.

Promotional engines include:

  • Achievers, or “play X, get Y” style promotions
  • PickX, or virtual drawings and sweepstakes for floor-wide, criteria and list-driven player groups
  • SportsPIX
  • Expedition
  • Minimum points
  • New member promotions
