HALo CRMx Gateway

Integrate HALo Loyalty Solutions to ACSC and Customized iSeries Systems

HALo CRMx Gateway is HALo’s primary gateway for promotional activity. It allows HALo CORE and HALo Promotions to issue awards fulfilled through Advanced Casino Systems Corporation (ACSC) or Customized iSeries slot management systems, such as Free Play, Points, and Comp Value in real time and in response to triggered promotional events or redeemed direct mail offers.
