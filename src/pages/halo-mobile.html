HALo Mobile

Managing Customer Connections On the Go

HALo Mobile enables your employees to more easily manage customer enrollment and access player data directly from the casino floor on iOS and Android mobile devices. The app provides secure mobile access to all loyalty program player-management transactions and functionality across the enterprise.

With HALo Mobile, your frontline employees can provide better and more immediate customer support by:

  • Enrolling new players into your loyalty club right on the floor
  • Verifying legal age by scanning driver’s license bar codes
  • Viewing, updating, and adjusting player profiles, buckets and tiers
  • Developing players through issuing comps and incentives
  • Performing enterprise player searches
  • Accessing account history and player’s club functions
  • Contact management

HaLo Mobile puts the player, program and performance data necessary to serve your customers directly in the hands of the employees empowered to support your operations.
