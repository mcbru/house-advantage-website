HALo Kiosk

Self-Service Access to the Loyalty Experience

HALo Kiosk is a loyalty management solution for both enterprise and single site properties that empower customers to access, redeem, and manage their own experience with your loyalty program – at their convenience – through self-service kiosks, mobile apps, and secure web portals. Customers can quickly manage their player loyalty accounts, replace lost cards, redeem offers and play entertaining, interactive promotional games without standing in long lines.

HALo Kiosk gives players self-service access to functionality such as:

  • Reprint card
  • Update player information
  • View and redeem offers, comps and promotions
  • Request win/loss statement
  • View player balances and tier progress/status
  • Activate and/or print sweepstakes entries
  • Win awards by playing animated games

HALo Kiosk can also serve as an alternative marketing channel for personalized player messages based on targeted promotions, tier status, and player history.
